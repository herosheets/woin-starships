Electronic Warfare

Electronic warfare generally takes two forms. Offensive EA equipment includes ion beams and computer systems (bought elsewhere); defensive EA systems include jammers, countermeasures, encryption, and electronic reinforcement. Without electronic defensive measures, ships are very vulnerable to electronic attack which can drain CPU resources and shut down systems.
