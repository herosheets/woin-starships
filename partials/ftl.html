FTL Propulsion Systems

The existing engines cannot be sold or exchanged; they have an effective value of zero. There are many types of starship propulsion and power systems. Listed below are some common civilian systems, from liquid fuel or fusion systems to more advanced antimatter or hyperdrive engines.
